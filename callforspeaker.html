Call for Speaker

English

The Italian Community Managers Summit returns to Milan on November 15th to continue discussing, learning and improving the art of Community Management!

Do you want to share your experience in community management with dozens of other community managers?

Do you have a story to tell other community managers?

We are ready to listen to you! We are looking for speakers and workshops facilitators for this edition of the ICM Summit.

Topic suggestions
  • a successful community case
  • tools, techniques and methodologies that can be useful to a community manager
  • strategies that worked, or didn't work
  • topics about the general sensetemi che riguardano il community management in senso generale: carriera, formazione, esperienze personali.
Some tips for the proposal
  • go straight to the point while presenting your talk: what's the message you want to communicate?
  • Highlight how your topics can be beneficial for a community manager. Mix and match theory and practice
  • Telling your experience is not enough. Try to extract lessons learned and principles useful also for other community managers
What is a talk?

A 30-minute presentation (25 minutes of speech + 5 minutes for Q&A)

What is a workshop?

A practical activity of about 1 hour in which as facilitator you will actively involve the participants in group work.

Language

Both Italian and English are welcome

Call for Speakers deadline

The Call for Speakers will close on September 15th. The ICM team will analyze the proposals and give you an answer after this deadline.

What are you waiting for? This is your occasion!

Hai voglia di condividere la tua esperienza nella gestione di una community?

Hai una storia da raccontare ad altri community manager?

Siamo pronti ad ascoltarti! Stiamo cercando speaker per talk e workshop per questa edizione di ICM Summit.

Temi che puoi affrontare
  • un caso di successo della tua community
  • strumenti, tecniche e metodologie
  • strategie che hanno o non hanno funzionato
  • temi che riguardano il community management in senso generale: carriera, formazione, esperienze personali.
Qualche consiglio per la stesura della proposta
  • cerca di presentarci il tuo speech andando dritto al punto. Che messaggio vuoi comunicare?

  • raccontare la tua esperienza non basta. Cerca di tirare fuori principi ed insegnamenti che possono valere anche per altri

Cos’è un workshop?
